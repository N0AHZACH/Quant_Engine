# Success Criteria — Locked Before Backtesting

PRIMARY (NON-NEGOTIABLE):
- Max drawdown ≤ 20%
- Drawdown recovery ≤ 6 months

SECONDARY:
- Sharpe ratio ≥ 1.8
- CAGR ≥ 15%

TERTIARY:
- Turnover efficiency
- Regime robustness
- Stability across rolling windows

RULE:
These metrics are NOT allowed to change after results are observed.
